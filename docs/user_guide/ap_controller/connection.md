# Connect

## Main Router and AP Controller Mode

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/ap_controller/connection1.webp"  alt="" style="width:600px">

1. Connect the provided power adapter to the power jack of C200P.
2. Connect the WAN port of C200P to the Internet.
3. Connect one of the LAN ports of C200P to a switch.
4. Connect the APs and the management device to the switch.

!!! Note
    A maximum of 10 APs or 100 clients are allowed in this mode.

----

## AP Controller Mode

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/ap_controller/connection2.webp"  alt="" style="width:600px">

1. Connect the provided power adapter to the power jack of C200P.
2. Connect the WAN port of C200P directly or through a switch to a router with the Internet.
3. Connect one of the LAN ports of C200P to the management device.
4. Connect the APs to the switch or directly to the rest LAN ports of C200P.

!!! Note
    A maximum of 200 APs are allowed in this mode.
