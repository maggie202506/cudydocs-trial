## Interfaces
| <span style="font-size: 1.2em;">Interface</span> | <span style="font-size: 1.2em;">Description</span> |
|-----------------------|-------------|
| **USB 3.0 Button**    | Connect to a USB device for data storage. |
|<nobr>**LAN (PoE OUT) Port**</nobr>| Connect to LAN devices or supply power for devices. <br><br>Note: It cannot supply power for other devices via PoE OUT ports <br>when itself powered by PoE device via PoE IN port.|
|<nobr>**WAN (PoE IN) Port**</nobr>| Connect to the Internet or be powered by a PoE device. |
| **Reset Button**       | Press for 2 seconds to restore the factory defaults. |
| **K-Slot**            | Secure with a durable cable to a fixed object to prevent theft. |
| **DC Power Jack**      | Connect to a power adapter of 48V~57V for power supply. |
| **Ground Connector**   | Attach to the ground wire for safety and reliability. |

## LEDs
| <span style="font-size: 1.2em;">LED</span> | <span style="font-size: 1.2em;">Description</span> |
|-----------------------|-------------|
|**System**| Off: No power. <br>Flash: Initializing or upgrading. <br>On: System already started.|
|**PoE Max**| Off: PoE output < 90% of PoE budget <br>Flash: 90% of PoE budget ≤ PoE output ≤ 95% of PoE budget <br>On: PoE output > 95% of PoE budget |
|**Link/Act** <br> (left LEDs)| Off: No link. <br>Flash: Transmitting data. <br>On: Link but no activity|
|**PoE** <br> (right LEDs)| Off: Not supplying power. <br>On: Supplying power.|
